Corn Pudding

Description

Corn pudding is the best of the best comfort food side dish. Creamy, sweet, and delicious it makes the perfect addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 6 cups corn

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease 2 quart casserole dish.
  2. Whisk eggs in large bowl. Add milk, melted butter, sugar, and cornstarch. Whisk until well combined. Stir in corn until fully blended. Pour into prepared dish.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until golden, about 1 hour.
